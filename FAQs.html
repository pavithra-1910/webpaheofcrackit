Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is the app about?

This app is designed to help users prepare for placements by offering various features like mock interviews, technical questions, and interview tips.

How can I contact support?

You can contact support via the "Contact Support" option in the app. You can also reach us by email at support@placementapp.com.

Is my data secure on this app?

Yes, your data is stored securely. We use encryption techniques to protect your personal information and ensure that it is safe from unauthorized access.

How can I provide feedback or suggestions?

We love hearing from our users! You can provide feedback or suggestions through the "Feedback" section in the app or by emailing us at feedback@placementapp.com.

What is the app version?

The current version of the app is 1.0.0. Check the "App Version" section in the settings to see if any updates are available.
